Import transactions directly from your bank's CSV export.

**Supported banks**

- Barclays, HSBC, Lloyds, Nationwide
- Starling, Monzo, Revolut
- Santander, Metro Bank
- Any bank (manual column mapping)

**How it works**

1. Export a CSV statement from your online banking.
2. Open the Import Wizard from Income or Expenses.
3. Drag & drop your CSV file (or browse).
4. The app auto-detects your bank format.
5. Review transactions and assign categories.
6. Confirm to import into your records.

**Key features**

- Auto-detection of 9 major UK banks
- Duplicate detection flags already-imported transactions
- Smart categorisation suggests SA103 categories
- Import history to view and undo past imports

**Tips**

- Download CSV (not PDF) from your bank.
- Include the full date range you need.
- Review suggested categories before confirming.
- Personal transactions can be skipped during import.
