This app connects to HMRC using secure OAuth 2.0 — you sign in on HMRC's own website and the app never sees your password.

**How it works**

1. Click **Connect & Verify** in Settings.
2. Your browser opens HMRC's Government Gateway.
3. You log in with **your** Government Gateway credentials.
4. HMRC asks whether you grant access to this app.
5. You choose **Grant authority** and return to the app.

**Password safety**

- Your password is entered only on HMRC's website.
- This app never sees or stores your password.
- The app receives only a limited-access token, and HMRC controls what it can do.

**To disconnect**

- In **Settings → HMRC**, use the **Disconnect** button, or
- Log in to HMRC and revoke the app's access there.

Making Tax Digital for Income Tax is mandatory from April 2026 (income over £50,000), April 2027 (over £30,000) and April 2028 (over £20,000).
