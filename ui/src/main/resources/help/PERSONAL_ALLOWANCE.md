The standard Personal Allowance is £12,570 — the amount of income you can earn before paying Income Tax.

**High earner taper**

- If your income exceeds £100,000, your allowance reduces by £1 for every £2 over the threshold.
- At £125,140 or above you have **no** Personal Allowance.
