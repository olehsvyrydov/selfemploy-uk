By checking the declaration boxes you confirm the information you have provided is accurate and complete.

**Important**

- HMRC may check your figures and records.
- Penalties may apply for inaccurate returns.
- You are responsible for the accuracy of your submission.

Keep your records for at least **5 years** after the submission deadline, in case HMRC opens an enquiry.
