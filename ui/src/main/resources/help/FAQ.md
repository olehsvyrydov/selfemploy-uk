**Do I need to be registered as self-employed?**
Yes. You must be registered with HMRC and have a UTR.

**What tax years are supported?**
UK tax years from 2024/25 onwards.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes, for recording income and expenses. Internet is needed only for HMRC submissions.

**What if I make a mistake?**
Before submitting, edit or delete the entry. After submitting, contact HMRC to make corrections.

**Does this handle VAT?**
No. This app is for Income Tax and National Insurance (Class 2 and Class 4).

**Why doesn't my tax match my accountant's figure?**
Accountants may apply reliefs or allowances you have not entered. Always verify with a qualified professional.
