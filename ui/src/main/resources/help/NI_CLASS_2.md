Class 2 National Insurance helps you qualify for the State Pension and certain benefits.

**Since 6 April 2024**

- If your profits are **above** the Small Profits Threshold ({{class2SPT}}), you are treated as having **paid** Class 2 — you get the pension qualifying year without paying anything.
- If your profits are **below** the Small Profits Threshold, Class 2 is **voluntary**. You can choose to pay it ({{class2WeeklyRate}} a week for {{taxYear}}) to protect your State Pension entitlement.

This app applies the current rules for the tax year you are viewing.
