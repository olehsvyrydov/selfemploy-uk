Unpaid income is money you are owed but have not yet received.

This includes:

- Outstanding invoices
- Pending client payments
- Amounts owed to you

Tracking unpaid income helps you monitor cash flow, chase late payments, and plan expected revenue.

Note: on the **cash basis**, unpaid income is not counted towards your tax-year turnover until you receive it.
