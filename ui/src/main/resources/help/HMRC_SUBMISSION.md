When you submit, your data is sent securely to HMRC over the Making Tax Digital (MTD) API, HMRC processes it, and you receive a confirmation reference. Keep a copy for your records.

**Making Tax Digital for Income Tax — who and when**

| From | Qualifying income over |
|------|------------------------|
| April 2026 | £50,000 |
| April 2027 | £30,000 |
| April 2028 | £20,000 |

Under MTD you send **quarterly updates** of your income and expenses, then a **final declaration** after the tax year to confirm the figures and pay any tax due.
