Class 4 contributions are paid on your self-employment profits, as part of your Self Assessment tax bill.

**{{taxYear}} rates**

| Profits | Rate |
|---------|------|
| Below £12,570 | 0% |
| £12,570 – £50,270 | 6% |
| Over £50,270 | 2% |
