Only expenses **wholly and exclusively** for business purposes are tax-deductible.

Mixed personal/business expenses must be apportioned — you can only claim the business portion.

Examples of allowable expenses:

- Office supplies and equipment
- Business travel (not commuting)
- Professional subscriptions
- Accountancy fees
- Business insurance
