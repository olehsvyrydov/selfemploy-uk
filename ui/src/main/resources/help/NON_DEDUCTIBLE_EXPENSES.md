Some business costs cannot be claimed for tax relief. Record them for accurate accounting, but they will not reduce your tax bill.

Common non-deductible expenses:

- Business entertainment (client meals, hospitality)
- Depreciation (use Capital Allowances instead)
- Fines and penalties
- Personal expenses
- Political donations

Note: some costs may be **partially** deductible if they have a genuine business portion.
