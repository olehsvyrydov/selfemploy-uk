Paid income is money you have actually received for goods or services.

This includes:

- Invoice payments received
- Cash payments collected
- Bank transfers completed
- Cheques that have cleared

**Cash basis is the default**

Since the 2024/25 tax year, the **cash basis** (income recorded when received) is the default for most self-employed people. You can opt out and use *traditional accounting* (income recorded when earned) if it suits your business.
