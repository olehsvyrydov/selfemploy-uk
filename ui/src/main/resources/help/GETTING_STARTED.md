Welcome to UK Self-Employment Manager — a free, privacy-first application for UK self-employed individuals.

Here is a quick overview of what it does.

**What it does**

- **Track income** — record all business income
- **Track expenses** — log expenses by SA103 category
- **Calculate tax** — real-time Income Tax and National Insurance estimates
- **Submit to HMRC** — Making Tax Digital submission

**First steps**

1. Complete the setup wizard with your details.
2. Add your first income entry from the Dashboard.
3. Record business expenses as you incur them.
4. Review your Tax Summary regularly.
5. Connect to HMRC when you are ready to submit.
