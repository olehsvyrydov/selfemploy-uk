If your tax bill is more than £1,000, you usually make advance payments towards next year's bill.

**How it works**

- Each payment is half of your previous year's tax bill.
- First payment: **31 January**.
- Second payment: **31 July**.

If you have paid too much you get a refund; if you have not paid enough you make a *balancing payment*.
