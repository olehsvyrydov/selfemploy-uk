The Software and its documentation are provided for informational purposes only. Nothing contained in the Software should be construed as:

  • Professional tax advice
  • Legal advice
  • Financial planning advice
  • A substitute for consultation with qualified professionals

Bank Statement Import

The Software's bank statement import feature includes automatic categorisation suggestions. These suggestions:

  • Are not tax advice and should not be relied upon without verification
  • May incorrectly categorise transactions
  • Do not account for your specific tax circumstances
  • Must be reviewed and verified by you before use in any HMRC submission

Incorrect categorisation of expenses or income on your Self Assessment return may result in HMRC penalties. We strongly recommend consulting a qualified accountant if you are unsure how to categorise a transaction.

Accuracy of Calculations

While we make reasonable efforts to ensure tax calculations are accurate and up-to-date:

  • Tax laws and rates change frequently
  • Individual circumstances vary significantly
  • Complex situations may not be fully covered
  • Errors may exist in the Software

Always verify calculations independently before relying on them for tax submissions or financial decisions.
