Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

Is this software really free?

Yes! UK Self-Employment Manager is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases. The software is open-source under the Apache 2.0 License, meaning the code is publicly available and the software will remain free forever.

Is my data secure?

Your data security is our top priority. All your financial information is stored locally on your computer in an encrypted database. We never have access to your data - there are no cloud servers, no data collection, and no tracking. When you submit to HMRC, your data is sent directly to HMRC's official servers using their secure MTD API, never through our systems.

Can I submit directly to HMRC?

Yes! The software integrates directly with HMRC's Making Tax Digital (MTD) API, allowing you to submit your Self Assessment return without leaving the application. You'll authenticate securely through the HMRC Government Gateway, and your submission will be sent directly to HMRC. You'll receive confirmation and a receipt for your records.

Who is this software for?

This software is designed for UK self-employed individuals and sole traders who need to file Self Assessment tax returns. It's ideal for:

  • Freelancers and contractors
  • Small business owners
  • Gig economy workers
  • Anyone who wants to manage their taxes independently

If you're employed and only need to file a simple tax return, or if you're a limited company, this software may not be the best fit for you.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?

Not at all! We've designed the software to be simple and intuitive, with no accounting or technical knowledge required. The interface guides you through each step, and helpful tooltips explain everything in plain English. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can use this software.

Can I trust the tax calculations?

Our tax calculations are verified against HMRC guidelines and updated for each tax year. The software calculates Income Tax bands, Personal Allowance, National Insurance Class 2 and Class 4 contributions according to current UK tax law. However, we always recommend consulting with a qualified accountant for complex tax situations. The software includes a disclaimer that it provides guidance but not professional tax advice.

How do I report bugs or request features?

We welcome community feedback! Please open an issue on our GitHub repository. For bug reports, please include steps to reproduce the issue and any error messages. For feature requests, describe the use case and how it would help you. We also accept code contributions - see our contribution guidelines.

What tax years are supported?

The software currently supports the 2024/25 and 2025/26 tax years, with tax rates and thresholds updated for each year. We release updates before each new tax year to ensure you always have accurate calculations. Previous tax year support is maintained for historical reference and late submissions.
