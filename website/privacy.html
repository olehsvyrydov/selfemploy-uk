Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

UK Self-Employment Manager is designed with privacy as a core principle. Unlike cloud-based accounting software, your financial data never leaves your computer. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal or financial information.

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your computer in an encrypted database. This includes:

  • Income and expense records
  • Tax calculations
  • Business information
  • HMRC submission history

We never upload, sync, or backup your data to any external servers.

HMRC Integration

When you submit your Self Assessment to HMRC:

  • Your data is sent directly to HMRC's official MTD API servers
  • Authentication is handled through the HMRC Government Gateway
  • We do not act as an intermediary - the connection is directly between your device and HMRC
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely on your device and can be revoked at any time

Website Analytics

This website (selfemploy.uk) may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand visitor patterns. We do not:

  • Track individual users
  • Use cookies for advertising
  • Share data with third parties for marketing

Open Source Transparency

As an open-source project, our entire codebase is publicly available for inspection. You can verify our privacy claims by reviewing the source code on GitHub.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: You have complete access to your data at all times
  • Portability: You can export your data in standard formats
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the application removes all data
  • Control: You decide what data to enter and when to submit to HMRC

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
