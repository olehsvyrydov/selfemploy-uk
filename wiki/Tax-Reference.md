## SA103 Form Mapping

The application aligns expense categories with HMRC Self Assessment form SA103F (Self-Employment Full).

### Income Boxes

| SA103 Box | Field Name | Description |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **Box 9** | Turnover | Total business income/sales |
| **Box 10** | Other business income | Grants, Covid support, other |

### Expense Categories

| SA103 Box | Category | Description | Deductible |
|-----------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Box 10** | Cost of Sales | Goods bought for resale | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 11** | Construction Costs | Subcontractor costs | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 12** | Staff Costs | Wages, salaries, pensions | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 13** | Travel | Car, van, public transport | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 14** | Premises | Rent, rates, power, insurance | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 15** | Repairs | Property and equipment maintenance | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 16** | Office Costs | Phone, stationery, software | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 17** | Advertising | Marketing, business entertainment | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 18** | Interest | Bank and business loan interest | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 19** | Financial Charges | Bank fees, credit card charges | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 20** | Bad Debts | Irrecoverable debts written off | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 21** | Professional Fees | Accountant, legal fees | ✅ Yes |
| **Box 22** | Depreciation | Asset depreciation | ❌ No |
| **Box 23** | Other Expenses | Other business expenses | ✅ Yes |

### Profit Calculation

| SA103 Box | Calculation |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Box 26** | Total allowable expenses (sum of deductible categories) |
| **Box 27** | Net profit = Box 9 + Box 10 - Box 26 |
| **Box 28** | Net loss (if Box 27 is negative) |
| **Box 29** | Adjusted profit (after capital allowances) |

## Income Tax Rates (2025/26)

### Tax Bands

| Band | Income Range | Rate |
|------|--------------|------|
| Personal Allowance | £0 - £12,570 | 0% |
| Basic Rate | £12,571 - £50,270 | 20% |
| Higher Rate | £50,271 - £125,140 | 40% |
| Additional Rate | Over £125,140 | 45% |

### Personal Allowance Taper

For income over £100,000:
- Personal allowance reduces by £1 for every £2 over £100,000
- At £125,140, personal allowance is fully withdrawn

### Calculation Example

**Taxable Profit: £60,000**

```
Personal Allowance: £12,570 (0%)     = £0
Basic Rate:         £37,700 (20%)    = £7,540
Higher Rate:        £9,730  (40%)    = £3,892
                                      -------
Total Income Tax:                      £11,432
```

## National Insurance Class 4 (2025/26)

### Rates

| Band | Profit Range | Rate |
|------|--------------|------|
| Below Lower Profits Limit | £0 - £12,570 | 0% |
| Main Rate | £12,570 - £50,270 | 6% |
| Additional Rate | Over £50,270 | 2% |

### Calculation Example

**Taxable Profit: £60,000**

```
Below LPL: £12,570      (0%)  = £0
Main Rate: £37,700      (6%)  = £2,262
Additional: £9,730      (2%)  = £195
                              -------
Total NI Class 4:              £2,457
```

## Allowable Expenses

### Definitely Allowable ✅

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Office costs** | Stationery, phone bills, software subscriptions |
| **Travel** | Fuel, parking, train tickets, mileage |
| **Staff** | Salaries, employer NI, pension contributions |
| **Stock** | Raw materials, goods for resale |
| **Professional fees** | Accountant, solicitor, professional subscriptions |
| **Marketing** | Advertising, website costs |
| **Insurance** | Business insurance, professional indemnity |
| **Financial** | Bank charges, loan interest |

### NOT Allowable ❌

| Category | Reason |
|----------|--------|
| **Personal expenses** | Not wholly for business |
| **Entertainment** | Specifically disallowed by HMRC |
| **Fines and penalties** | Public policy |
| **Depreciation** | Use Capital Allowances instead |
| **Clothing** | Unless specific uniform/costume |
| **Home costs** | Unless using simplified expenses |
